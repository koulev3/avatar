# Avatar Legends: Kyoshi Era Campaign Guide

## Campaign Setup
- **System:** Avatar Legends RPG
- **Era:** Kyoshi Era
- **Core Dynamic:** Fire Nation influence is expanding subtly through diplomacy, commerce, legal pressure, and local proxies rather than overt imperial conquest.
- **Regional Pressure:** Border communities absorb the first shocks of imbalance before capitals react.

## Foundation Event (Session 0)
### The Tea Incident
The player characters were present in an Earth Kingdom coastal village when a Fire Nation officer publicly punished a local child.

The punishment was unjust but legally ambiguous, and it weaponized tea as a social symbol of balance, hospitality, respect, and cultural continuity.

No one in the village intervened in time, including witnesses with the ability to act.

This event is campaign canon and should continue to evolve through rumors, memory, politics, and consequence.

## Tone and Table Priorities
- Moral ambiguity over clean hero/villain binaries
- Quiet injustice over spectacle-first cruelty
- Cultural friction at personal and civic scales
- Political fallout from small, local decisions
- Consequences from both action and inaction

## GM Style Defaults
- Start every session and scene **in media res**
- Avoid passive opening scenes and static exposition
- Maintain active pressure at all times
- Give every significant NPC an agenda
- Propagate consequences forward across sessions

## Live Campaign Tracks
Update these after each major scene and at session end:

1. **Political Pressure**
   - Fire Nation posture: `observe -> influence -> control`
   - Earth Kingdom local response: fragmented cooperation, fear, resentment, resistance

2. **Community Sentiment**
   - Village posture: `fear -> resentment -> resistance`
   - Merchant and elder blocs may diverge

3. **PC Reputation**
   - Seen as helpers, meddlers, agitators, symbols, or threats

4. **Moral Balance Axes (Party + individual PCs)**
   - Justice vs Mercy
   - Action vs Restraint
   - Loyalty vs Truth

5. **Tea Incident Echoes**
   - Retellings, distortions, propaganda use, and symbolic copycat events

## Elemental Symbolism Lens
When generating scenes, NPC pressure, or complications, reinforce symbolic currents:
- **Fire:** control, ambition, escalation
- **Earth:** resistance, stability, stubbornness
- **Water:** emotion, adaptation, undercurrents
- **Air:** avoidance, freedom, detachment

## Session Prep Workflow
1. Identify current tensions, unresolved hooks, and who currently holds leverage.
2. Build a strong start in media res with immediate urgency.
3. Prepare scene pressures (not fixed outcomes).
4. Attach every major beat to one of:
   - Tea Incident origin
   - Tea Incident ripple
   - Fire Nation pressure response cycle
5. Pre-plan likely consequence updates (immediate, delayed, hidden).
6. Confirm at least one moral-pressure decision per PC.
